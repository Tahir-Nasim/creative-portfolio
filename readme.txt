A starter theme called creative-portfolio.

== Description ==

The Creative Portfolio WordPress Theme is a dynamic and versatile template designed to empower artists, designers, photographers, and creative professionals to showcase their work in a stunning and memorable way. With its sleek and contemporary design, this theme is the ideal choice for those who want to make a lasting impression and leave their mark in the digital world.  Key Features:  1. **Visually Stunning Portfolio**: The theme\'s core focus is on presenting your creative work beautifully. It offers various customizable layouts for showcasing your projects, photographs, artwork, and designs with utmost visual appeal.  2. **Intuitive Drag-and-Drop Builder**: Building and customizing your portfolio has never been easier. The intuitive drag-and-drop page builder lets you create and rearrange your content effortlessly, ensuring that your website reflects your unique style.  3. **Responsive Design**: Your portfolio will look and function flawlessly on all devices, from desktops to smartphones. This responsiveness ensures that your audience can enjoy your work anywhere, anytime.  4. **Diverse Portfolio Types**: Whether you\'re a photographer, graphic designer, illustrator, or any other creative professional, this theme supports various portfolio types, such as grid, masonry, slider, and more, to suit your specific needs.  5. **Rich Media Support**: Showcase your work in its true glory with support for various media types, including images, videos, and interactive elements. Let your creativity shine through.  6. **Customizable Typography**: Tailor the typography to match your brand or personal style. Choose from a vast array of fonts and font styles to make your website truly unique.  7. **Color Customization**: Easily adjust the color scheme to complement your work or brand identity. The theme provides a simple interface for selecting and customizing colors to suit your preferences.  8. **Built-in Blog**: Share your creative journey, insights, and updates with your audience through the theme\'s integrated blog feature. Engage with your visitors and build a community around your work.  9. **Social Media Integration**: Seamlessly connect your social media profiles to your website, making it effortless for visitors to follow your creative endeavors across various platforms.  10. **SEO-Friendly**: Improve your website\'s visibility in search engines with built-in SEO optimization tools. This theme ensures that your portfolio ranks well and reaches a broader audience.  11. **One-Click Demo Import**: Get started quickly by importing one of the theme\'s demo layouts. Then, customize it to your liking to create a unique online presence.  12. **Customer Support**: Receive dedicated support from the theme\'s developers to help you navigate any technical issues and make the most of its features.  With the Creative Portfolio WordPress Theme, you have the ultimate tool to exhibit your creativity, impress potential clients, and establish your online presence. Whether you\'re an emerging talent or an established artist, this theme will elevate your portfolio to new heights and ensure that your work gets the attention it deserves. Create a stunning online showcase that leaves a lasting impact with this exceptional theme.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this theme support any plugins? =

creative-portfolio includes support for WooCommerce and for Infinite Scroll in Jetpack.
